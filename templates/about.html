HISTORY OF Ahmad ElectroGas

Ahmad ElectroGas embarked on its journey in 1992, starting as a small distribution business with a passion for delivering quality products to the community.
Over the next decade,after 2005 we solidified our foundation and expanded our reach. A significant milestone came when we became the authorized dealer for two renowned national brands: Domesto Home Appliances and Golden Fuji. This partnership enabled us to offer a wider range of top-quality products to our customers.
After three decades, from 2020, Ahmad ElectroGas has evolved into a leading distributor of Home Appliances, partnering with multiple esteemed companies to bring the best products to your doorstep. Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and innovation continues to drive our growth and success.
Now we continue to evolve, we're excited to launch our website, allowing us to deliver our products directly to your doorstep.
